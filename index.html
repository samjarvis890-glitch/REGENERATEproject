Our Story

Crafted with
Purpose & Precision

Since 1984, Regenerate has stood as London's premier destination for vintage leather and fur restoration. What began as a single-artisan workshop in Mayfair has grown into a full-scale studio, guided by the belief that every heirloom deserves a second lifetime.

Our team of certified conservators blends time-honoured craft with modern ethical standards — sourcing only cruelty-free treatments, re-purposing vintage hardware, and partnering with sustainable suppliers.
