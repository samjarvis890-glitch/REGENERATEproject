Our Heritage

The Mayfair Origins

Begun as a single bench in a hidden London alleyway, our founder started by meticulously restitching torn lining in post-war aviation jackets, realizing the profound emotional connection people held to these garments.

Scientific Evolution

By the early 2000s, we integrated chemical conservators into our team. We stopped relying merely on traditional polishes and began engineering custom botanical lipid replenishing serums to permanently halt dry rot without residue.

The Modern Atelier

Today, Regenerate stands as the premier global facility for ethical vintage stabilization, trusted tightly by museums, royal archives, and private collectors worldwide to handle irretrievable mastercraft layers.

The Collective

Our atelier is home to twelve master conservators, each specializing in a distinct era of material history—from Edwardian fur stabilization to the restoration of mid-century aeronautical leathers. They are not merely technicians; they are the guardians of your heritage.
