01. Hide Rehydration

Using advanced fatliquoring techniques, we reverse brittle cracking by reintroducing natural animal matrix oils over a 14-day absorption protocol, restoring suppleness without altering grain.

02. Pigment Match

Exact color reconstruction achieved using period-accurate aniline dyes. We explicitly reject thick acrylic paints which suffocate hide pores and inevitably fracture over time.

03. Panel Splice

Irreversibly rotted sections are excised. We source matching vintage donor hides to splice in functional structural panels, meticulously tracing absolute original thread pathways.
